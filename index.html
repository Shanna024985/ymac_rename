Steps to rename yourself before Zoom Starts (Windows)

Step 1:

Open up zoom and click on to settings from your profile page

Step 2:

Click onto my account from the settings page

Step 3:

Click on edit my profile button

Step 4:

You will be redirected to this login screen in google. Log into your account that you are using for zoom here

Step 5:

After logging in, you will be shown this screen. Click on the edit button at the top right

Step 6:

Change your display name to the format shown here under the display name and click on save

Step 7:

Your display name has now been updated
